Ready to Submit a Job?

Photo Submission!

Submission requires a Google Account to upload and fill out the form. If you do not have a Google Account, please email me.

How do I submit?

You can click the submission button above and it will open a Google Form for you to fill out. At the end of it you can paste a cloud-drive link or upload a compressed file with all of your content in it!

The form will walk you through the options you have and let me know when you’re done and it’s uploaded.

Then What?

I’ll have a look at your content and make sure it got over correctly and confirm numbers with you. Once that’s done, I’ll send you an invoice based on your selections and other options we may have discussed.

I Don’t Have To Do This Every Time Right?!

Nope! If you like the service I can make a form that is pre-filled out for your information or even give you a dedicated upload folder and you can shoot me an email if anything changes. I often have folks that just need me to cull, once I know that you’d just need to let me know if anything changes.
